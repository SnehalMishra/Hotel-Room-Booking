About Us

ROYAL HOTEL encompasses entire world within its walls from comfortable and cozy rooms to soulful dining facilities. It caters to the needs of business travellers and leisure travellers. We at Hotel REDSTONE endeavour to make your visit a fantastic one. ROYAL HOTEL offers a memorable accommodation in a family environment. It has 10 tastefully decorated rooms with individual A/C units offering modern amenities like EPABX, Colour T.V. with satellite channels, Internet facilities, running hot and cold water, finest room service, and refrigerator in all Big Super Deluxe rooms etc. The professionally managed hotel offers its guests, personalized services that are not just best but are legendary. Our well-mannered staff takes care of all needs of the guest to make them delighted. It promises the best spirit of guest care and service in eco-friendly environment.
